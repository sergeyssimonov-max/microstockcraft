ABOUT

Kia ora! Let me introduce…

My name is Vera, and I’m a freelance photographer, illustrator, and picture researcher. Since 2009 I’ve sold my images and illustrations through major microstock agencies — Shutterstock, iStock, Dreamstime, Adobe Stock, Alamy, Depositphotos, 123RF, VectorStock, and others — as well as regional agencies such as Pixta (Japan) and KeyImagery (New Zealand).

Alongside my regular services I take on custom projects for clients, primarily e-stores, and occasionally provide free infographics to nonprofit organisations.

I also follow the microstock market and sometimes write articles about different parts of it. If you have any questions about microstock writing, feel free to get in touch.
